Park smarter.

Much time and energy is wasted driving around looking for a parking spot.

Spotz merges together parking data scattered over the internet to give you a comprehensive picture of where to most effectively find a parking spot. Our app shows a map of your surrounding area and overlays parking information. Users can contribute by taking pictures of parking signs when they park.

This app is currently in development and only includes Berkeley, CA.
